My Favorite Video Game

My favorite video game is Warframe

What is Warframe?

  • It is a completely free to play third person massive multiplayer online looter shooter
  • You play as a space ninja called a warframe
  • Has one of the best cinematic story quests in a video game
  • Has an amazing soundtrack
  • Has 10+ years of content

What can you do?

  • Hop in missions from planet to planet
  • Can have space ship battles
  • Can play through open worlds in various planets
  • Trade with other players in a player-driven market
  • Collect and upgrade warframes and weapons
  • And much more
